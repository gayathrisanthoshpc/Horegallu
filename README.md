# Horegallu

> A resting stone for those who carry too much.

**Horegallu** is a private, culturally grounded stress relief companion built for anyone—but especially for nursing students and healthcare workers who carry both academic pressure and emotional trauma.

The name comes from a Kannada word meaning a large flat stone placed on roadsides in rural India. A place for tired travelers to put their load down, rest for a moment, and continue their journey. That's what this app is.

---

## 🌿 The Problem

Most stress apps fail because they are generic, corporate, and forgotten after one use. When someone is stressed, their brain is already overwhelmed—the last thing they need is more menus, more choices, more reading.

Horegallu is different. It's honest about what it can and cannot fix:
- ✅ It helps manage stress in the moment
- ✅ It helps you understand what you're carrying  
- ✅ It helps you take one small action
- ✅ **It tells you when an app isn't enough**

**It is a painkiller, not surgery.** For the deeper problems—burnout, serious anxiety, relationship pain—you need human connection, real conversations, and sometimes professional help. Horegallu guides you there.

---

## 👥 Who It's For

**Primary:** Nursing students, healthcare workers, and anyone in caregiving roles who are trained to take care of others but never trained to take care of themselves.

**Extended:** Anyone carrying too much. Stress and overwhelm are universal human experiences.

**All languages:** Available in English, Malayalam, and Hindi. Each language written fresh—not translated. The app feels native in each language.

## ✨ The Unique Design

Horegallu has seven design signatures you won't find in other stress apps:

1. **The Stone Breathes** — SVG gently inhales and exhales on a 5-second cycle. The visual itself calms.

2. **Kintsugi Gold Crack** — A pulsing golden crack glows through the stone. Broken things repaired with gold are more beautiful than unbroken ones. You are Kintsugi.

3. **Dust Particles** — 55 warm floating motes drift across the screen on a canvas layer. The screen feels alive.

4. **Weight Drop Animation** — When you choose your stress, the option drops to ground and others float away. The metaphor is the message: you put it down.

5. **Posture Morphing** — The person sitting on the stone shifts posture based on your stress type. Hunched for exhaustion. Head down for relationship pain. Looking up for the unknown.

6. **Handwritten Notes** — After you choose, a Caveat font message appears, as if left by someone who sat there before: "One step at a time." "You are more than you know."

7. **Earthy Palette** — Stone, terracotta, cream, sage, gold—the colors of a quiet road, late afternoon, a moment of stillness. Nothing clinical. Nothing corporate.

## 📋 The Ten Features

### Step 1: **Opening & Language Selection**
One question: "What are you carrying today?" in your chosen language. Six honest options written in real human language. The unique design identity lives here.

### Step 2: **Name It to Tame It**
Research shows naming an emotion precisely reduces its intensity. Horegallu names it:
- Study pressure → **Perfectionism Paralysis**
- Clinical trauma → **Compassion Fatigue**
- Not enough → **Impostor Syndrome**  
- Exhausted → **Burnout**
- Relationship pain → **Emotional Dysregulation**
- Overwhelmed → **Existential Anxiety**

Each emotion gets etymology, definition, neuroscience, and comfort.

### Step 3: **3am Mode**
Automatically activates 10pm–5am. Stars on canvas, moon SVG, dark warm palette, slower pace. Four working night tools:
- **4-7-8 Breathing** — Counts automatically. Proven to calm the nervous system.
- **5-4-3-2-1 Grounding** — Engage all five senses.
- **Body Scan** — Step through, release tension.
- **Thought Emptying** — Write everything. Release it forever.

Rechecks time every 60 seconds for live transition.

### Step 4: **Stress Translated**
Interactive body map. Five tappable hotspots (Brain, Heart, Lungs, Muscles, Gut). Real neuroscience explained simply. Animated hormone bars show the truth: Cortisol and Adrenaline spike, Dopamine and Serotonin drop. The message: **You are not broken. The system is working.**

### Step 5: **The Unsent Letter**
Paper-styled writing space with line rules and margin. Write to anyone—professor, patient's family, friend, yourself. Say everything you can't say out loud. AI reads with empathy, identifies your real need, asks gentle questions. Then burn it. Nothing is ever saved—in-memory only.

### Step 6: **Peer Wisdom**
18 authentic one-liners per language from nursing students, graduate nurses, night shift nurses, international students. Real human things like:
> "I cried in the hospital bathroom twice and still graduated."

Not motivational quotes. Real wisdom. Auto-rotates, filterable, users can leave their own word on the stone.

### Step 7: **What Do You Actually Need?**
Four sequential questions identify your real need:
1. What does this feeling want to do?
2. How long have you carried this?
3. Is there a person involved?
4. What does support look like to you?

Five possible needs identified with inline tools for each: breathing circle, write space, call script, cry permission, sit-with-it permission.

### Step 8: **Smallest Possible Next Step**
AI takes what feels impossible and finds ONE embarrassingly small action for the next 10 minutes. Not "study for an hour" but "open the textbook to page 1." With psychology behind why it works, time estimate, and comfort. Celebration screen when done.

### Step 9: **Burnout & Escalation Detection**
16 honest check items across four groups (Energy, Emotions, Work, Body). Weighted scoring. Crisis item auto-triggers escalation without tools—just real human guidance and crisis numbers:
- **iCall:** 9152987821
- **Vandrevala Foundation:** 1860-2662-345

Three honest result levels:
- **Managing** — Warm, practical, three helps
- **Early Burnout** — Direct, "this is a signal," real next steps
- **Escalation** — No tools. Just: "What you're carrying is too heavy for an app."

### Step 10: **The AI That Listens**
Full conversation interface. You vent about your specific situation. AI responds to exactly what you wrote—not templates. Warm, human, specific. The stone listens.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Open **index.html** in any web browser
2. Choose your language (English, Malayalam, or Hindi)
3. Answer the opening question honestly
4. Follow the feature that fits your need
5. Use multiple features if you want—they're designed to flow together

**No installation. No account. No data saved. No one watching.**

---

## 💻 Technical Stack

- **Frontend:** Vanilla JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3
- **Design:** SVG illustrations, Canvas particle effects, smooth CSS animations
- **Fonts:** Cormorant Garamond (serif/soul), DM Sans (body), Caveat (handwritten moments), Noto Sans Malayalam & Devanagari (native scripts)
- **Deployment:** Static HTML files (works on any web server or locally)
- **AI Integration:** Optional Claude API for features like unsent letter, emotion naming, smallest step

---

## 📱 Languages

- **English** — Full support
- **Malayalam** (മലയാളം) — Full support, native script rendering

## 🌍 Try It Now

The app works completely offline. Download the files and open `index.html` in your browser. No internet required for the core experience (AI features require API connection).
