## What is Horegallu?

In rural India, a *horegallu* is a large flat stone placed on the side of a road — specifically for tired travelers carrying heavy loads to sit down, rest, and breathe before continuing their journey.

This app is that stone.

Built for nursing students and anyone overwhelmed by life — not to fix your stress, but to give you a place to put it down for a moment. To understand what you are carrying. To find one small thing to do. And to know honestly when you need more than an app.

---

## ✨ What Makes It Different

Most stress apps feel corporate and cold. Horegallu was designed to feel human.

| Signature | What it does |
|---|---|
| 🪨 **The stone breathes** | Gentle inhale-exhale animation that slows the first tap |
| ✨ **Kintsugi gold crack** | A pulsing golden fissure that celebrates repair, not perfection |
| 🌫️ **Dust particles** | 55 warm floating motes — the page feels alive, not flat |
| ⬇️ **Weight drop** | Your chosen stress drops to the ground. You literally put it down |
| 🧍 **Posture morphing** | The sitter shifts depending on your stress type |
| ✍️ **Handwritten notes** | A personal message appears like a note left by someone who sat here before |
| 🎨 **Earthy palette** | Stone, terracotta, cream, sage, gold — nothing clinical |

## 🗂️ The Ten Features

### 1. Opening & Language Selection
One honest question: "What are you carrying today?" Six real stress options lead to a personal, language-native journey.

### 2. Name It to Tame It
Naming the stress reduces its weight.
- Study pressure → **Perfectionism Paralysis**
- Clinical trauma → **Compassion Fatigue**
- Not enough → **Impostor Syndrome**
- Exhausted → **Burnout**
- Relationship pain → **Emotional Dysregulation**
- Overwhelmed → **Existential Anxiety**

Each label includes meaning, comfort, and simple neuroscience.

### 3. 3am Mode
Night mode activates automatically from 10pm to 5am. Dark palette, stars, moon, slower pace, and four calming night tools:
- **4-7-8 Breathing**
- **5-4-3-2-1 Grounding**
- **Body Scan**
- **Thought Emptying**

### 4. Stress Translated
A tactile body map and hormone story show what your stress is doing inside you.

### 5. The Unsent Letter
Write to someone you can’t tell. AI reflects back what matters, then the letter is burned. Nothing is stored.

### 6. Peer Wisdom
18 human one-liners in each language from nursing students, nurses, night shift workers, and travelers.
> "I cried in the hospital bathroom twice and still graduated."

### 7. What Do You Actually Need?
Four guided questions identify your real need and offer the right next step.

### 8. Smallest Possible Next Step
AI turns overwhelm into one embarrassingly small action for the next 10 minutes.

### 9. Burnout & Escalation Detection
A grounded burnout check that says when the app is enough and when it is not.

### 10. The AI That Listens
A full conversational space for what you want to say now. Warm, specific, and responsive.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in a browser
2. Choose your language
3. Answer the opening question honestly
4. Follow the feature that fits your moment
5. Use multiple features if you need them

**No install. No login. No tracking.**
